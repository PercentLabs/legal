1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The App does not require users to create an account and Percent Labs does not collect or store personal information on its own servers.

If you purchase a subscription through Google Play, Google securely processes your payment information. We do not receive or store your credit card details.

Device and Network Information

To provide remote control functionality, the App may access locally:

  • Your device's local network.
  • Your device's local IP address.

This information is used exclusively to discover and communicate with Roku devices on the same local network. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Percent Labs servers.

Google AdMob may collect and process certain information according to Google's own Privacy Policy, including:
